What is Indoor IoT?

Indoor IoT is the capability of devices to communicate with each other over one or more wireless networks.

This can include smartphones, connected light fixtures, connected sensors, door locks, inventory and smartwatches.

Gateways are a critical part of this set up, as they are devices built to bridge two or more networks that normally cannot communicate.

DNA Spaces enables the consolidated onboarding & configuration of devices, and connects them to industry specific applications to enable commercial outcomes.
